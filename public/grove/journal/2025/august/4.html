Scheduled to work 5PM-closing time today, so watching the new "The Wonderfully Weird World of Gumball" show. Just finished episode 2, and I gotta say this is one of few show 'sequels' or 'followups' that are as good if not better than the original, and that makes me really happy. My mom got the family into watching TAWOG a long time ago when new seasons were still airing and it became like part of the family culture, though since the finale we stopped really sitting down together for it since all episodes on cartoon network were reruns.

Started as a little family inside joke, a long time ago we'd watch the episodes as they came out on demand on our mom's old cable tv, and at some point during the ad segments, the same ad would play over and over across episodes' ad breaks, sometimes multiple times per episode's ad breaks. Eventually we got so tired of it that we just found it funny whenever it happened, and because the ads got stuck in our head we'd then think about it later, bringing up a quote or something. There were a few quotes. These were probably a stim for us.

So Ever since the first few times we saw the "Gumball Sewer Sweater Search" mobile game ad we have this bit where when we go to visit mom's or even just talking to her, say unexpectedly or throw into a setup to a joke - "gumball's in a sewer on a search for a sweather", and that was our special thing. It even became competitive where per-weekend we visit, the first person to say it 'gets it', and you can't say it too soon, not immediately during the car ride, and all 3 of me, mom, and my brother, have to be present for it to 'count'.

Stupid but funny, it makes me miss those visitation weekends, I haven't actually been over there since my birthday last year.
